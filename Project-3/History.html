History of the Brand

In 2010, founders Khushi Patel embarked on a mission to redefine sustainable living by breathing life into discarded items. Witnessing the environmental impact of waste and inspired by the beauty that could emerge from unwanted gifts, they established Recycle Your Ugly Gifts. At the heart of their vision was the idea of transforming waste into valuable treasures. Recycle Your Ugly Gifts pioneered the concept of creating stylish, functional products using discarded materials. From old textiles to forgotten trinkets, every item had the potential to be reborn through thoughtful upcycling.As the brand gained momentum, Recycle Your Ugly Gifts became synonymous with eco-chic living. Their curated collections featured everything from fashionable accessories crafted from reclaimed fabrics to home decor made from repurposed materials, setting new standards for sustainable style.

Founders

Khushi Patel, the visionary founder of Recycle Your Ugly Gifts,, is a counterpart in the areas of conscious consumerism and ecological living. Gifted with an interest in environmental conservation and an insightful perception of the potential hidden in abandoned goods, Khushi set out to transform our understanding of garbage in 2010. Driven by a desire to address the environmental impact of discarded items, Khushi sought to transform this overlooked "ugliness" into something beautiful and purposeful. With an unwavering commitment to the principles of repurposing and upcycling, she co-founded Recycle Your Ugly Gifts as a testament to the transformative power of conscious design.
Today, Khushi Patel stands as a driving force behind Recycle Your Ugly Gifts, shaping the brand's identity as a beacon of style, sustainability, and the art of turning "ugly" into something extraordinary. Her vision continues to inspire individuals to make mindful choices, fostering a brighter and greener future for generations to come.
